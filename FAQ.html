    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything You Need to Know....is Below!

    Goldstone Gym & Fitness is one of the biggest gyms in Ireland, offering state of the art equipment and facilities over 32,000 sq ft of floor space.

    HOW DO I JOIN PRIME FITNESS?

    It’s a really simple registration process and it’s all handled here on our website or via kiosks located at the entrance to our gym. Joining Goldstone will take you 5 minutes tops!


    WHAT MEMBERSHIPS ARE AVAILABLE?

    You can choose from a wide variety of membership types designed to suit your lifestyle. to reduce your monthly membership price with a 6 or 12-month membership package, we've got something to suit you.


    HOW DO I PAY?

    You can decide to pay for your membership monthly via direct debit or all at once as a lump sum.


    ARE ALL THE CLASSES FREE?

    The vast majority of our most popular classes, but some speciality classes incur a small charge. You can view your local gym's class timetable here.


    HOW DO I CANCEL MY MEMBERSHIP?

    WITHIN THE FIRST 10 DAYS If you are within 10 days of your joining date and have only visited the club once, you can still change your mind. If you decide you'd like to leave within this time,. Please contact your HR department.


    DO YOU PROVIDE TOWELS?

    In some gyms we offer free towels for our members, simply pick one up from reception on your way in.


    You can request a new gym card at your home club. There will be a charge for a replacement card due to loss or damage.


    HOW DO I CANCEL A CLASS

    You can cancel a class booking anytime from booking and up to two hours before the class starts.
